Base Powers

  • Circle: Energy Manipulation - Harness and control various forms of energy.
  • Triangle: Precision/Sharpness - Enhance accuracy and cutting power.
  • Square: Defense/Shielding - Create protective barriers and fortifications.
  • Star: Explosive Force - Unleash devastating explosive attacks.
  • Spiral: Space/Time Control - Manipulate the fabric of space and time.
  • Hexagon: Elemental Mastery - Command the elements of nature.
  • Pentagon: Speed/Agility - Move with incredible swiftness and agility.
  • Crescent: Illusion/Deception - Create convincing illusions to deceive enemies.
  • Diamond: Crystal Power - Utilize the strength and properties of crystals.
  • Octagon: Strength/Endurance - Enhance physical strength and stamina.
  • Cross: Healing/Restoration - Restore health and vitality.
  • Wave: Sound/Vibration - Manipulate sound waves and vibrations.
  • Arrow: Accuracy/Tracking - Never miss a target with enhanced precision.
  • Cloud: Weather Control - Control and influence weather patterns.
  • Flame: Fire Manipulation - Command the power of fire.
  • Drop: Water Manipulation - Control and shape water.
  • Lightning: Electric Surge - Harness the power of electricity.
  • Skull: Necromancy/Darkness - Command the forces of darkness and the undead.
  • Heart: Emotion Influence - Influence and manipulate emotions.
  • Infinity: Limitless Potential - Unlock unlimited possibilities.

  • Thunder Sprinter: Pentagon + Lightning - Move with the speed of lightning.

  • Sonic Inferno: Flame + Wave - Unleash a fiery sonic boom.
  • Empathic Healer: Cross + Heart - Heal allies and influence their emotions.
  • Storm Archer: Arrow + Cloud - Command storms with precise strikes.
  • Infinite Tide Ruler: Infinity + Drop - Control endless tides and water currents.
  • Illusive Titan: Octagon + Crescent - A titan shrouded in illusions.
  • Nova Burst: (Single) Star - Unleash a burst of stellar energy.
  • Chrono Shifter: (Single) Spiral - Shift through time and space.
  • Dark Thunder Pyre: Lightning + Skull + Flame - A dark pyre fueled by thunder.
  • Eternal Velocity Surge: Circle + Pentagon + Infinity - Endless speed and energy.
  • Crystal Sanctuary: Square + Diamond + Cross - A healing crystal sanctuary.
  • Tempest Marksman: Wave + Arrow + Cloud - Precise storm strikes.
  • Aqua Empath: Heart + Drop + Hexagon - Emotional water elemental control.
  • Electric Precision Strike: Triangle + Lightning + Star - Precise electric blasts.
  • Infernal Time Reaper: Flame + Spiral + Skull - A time-reaping inferno.
  • Boundless Shadow Colossus: Crescent + Infinity + Octagon - An endless shadow titan.
